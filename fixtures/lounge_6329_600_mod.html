I think about that show often. Queen had just released their second album, and Mott the Hoople was at its peak of popularity. Yeah, it was excellent. Another outstanding concert I attended was December 2, 1972 at Cowtown Ballroom in KCMO. Steely Dan was the opening act for Frank Zappa on his Petit Wazoo tour. There were two shows that night. I went to the early one.

At the late show in Kansas City (12/2/72), an audience member yelled out the request “#### on stage!” Frank Zappa replied,

a) “No, no. Go see Alice Cooper.”
b) “Spend fifteen years learning to play a musical instrument, you come to Kansas City and the guy says ‘#### on stage!’”
c) “The name of this song is ‘Cosmik Debris’, which is as good as shitting on stage.”
d) “I just did #### on stage, and you missed it.”
e) all of the above

The answer is e.

Correct!

That is KC music lore. BTW, the opening act advertised for that concert was Tim Buckley. I have a flyer for the show. A few years ago I scanned it and sent the image to some guy who runs a Zappa website.
